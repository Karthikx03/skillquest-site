Frequently Asked Questions

Is the lottery really free to enter?
Yes, completely free. As long as you're in the top 100 on the leaderboard at the end of the month, you're automatically entered. No points to spend, no tickets to buy.
How are winners picked?
One winner is drawn randomly from each tier (Platinum: top 10, Gold: rank 11–50, Silver: rank 51–100) on the last day of every month. A learner can only win one tier per month.
When and how are prizes delivered?
Winners are notified by email within 24 hours of the draw. Gift card codes (Shopee or Amazon, winner's choice) are sent directly to the registered email address within 3 business days.
Can I win multiple tiers in the same month?
No — each learner can only win one prize per month. If you rank in the top 10 you're entered in the Platinum draw only. If you win, you're excluded from lower tiers that month.
My rank changed near the end of the month — which rank counts?
The leaderboard snapshot taken at 23:59 (Taiwan time, UTC+8) on the last day of the month determines eligibility. Keep earning points right up until the deadline!
