Frequently Asked Questions

Draw rules and eligibility

Is the draw really free to enter?
Yes, completely free. There are no tickets to purchase and no SkillQuest points to spend. Eligibility is based entirely on your position on the public leaderboard at the close of each month.
How are winners selected?
One winner is drawn at random from each tier on the last day of the month. A learner can only win one tier per month. If you rank in the top 10, you are entered only in the Platinum draw.
When and how are gift cards delivered?
Winners are notified by email within 24 hours of the draw. Gift card codes — Shopee or Amazon, your choice — are sent to your registered email address within 3 business days.
Which rank counts if mine changes near month end?
The leaderboard snapshot taken at 23:59 Taiwan time (UTC+8) on the final day of the month is used to determine eligibility and tier placement. Keep earning points right up to the deadline.
Can I win in multiple tiers in the same month?
No. You are entered in only one tier based on your final rank. If you are in the top 10, you are a Platinum entrant only. If you win, you are excluded from all lower tiers that month.
